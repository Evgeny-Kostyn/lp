A Partner You Can Rely On

For the past 9 years, we have been developing and supporting premium themes, and there aren’t any other devs who can boast such an extensive portfolio of well-maintained themes.
High Capacity
We have a large team that can be dynamically redistributed — for example, we can pause development on one project and shift focus to addressing bug fixes in other projects. We are highly adaptable in our work and are capable to update all our portfolio within just a few hours.
Redundant Capacity of Teams
Our teams are divided into pairs, which allows us to be flexible — there is always a replacement available for a team member, whether they are on vacation or in other circumstances, unlike smaller independent theme authors.
Expansive Support Team
Thanks to our extensive support, we are always available and respond promptly to customer inquiries, resolving their issues efficiently.
